Why £8?

Every generation has a pint price that feels outrageous. £1 in the 80s. £3 in the 2000s. £5 in the 2020s.

The next psychological barrier? £8 for the average pint.

While London drinkers already see £7–£8 pints regularly, the UK average is still around £5. This clock tracks how close we are to the first £8 average pint across Britain.
